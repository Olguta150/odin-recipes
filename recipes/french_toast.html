Fluffy French Toast

Yummy French Toast

This French toast recipe is different because it uses flour. I have given it to some friends and they've all liked it better than French toast they usually make!



Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pich salt
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.

  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat
  3. Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on the each side golden brown. Serve hot.
