Waffle

A lovely, crispy waffle perfect for the morning.

Delicious, easy to make, loved by everyone. You can't go wrong with this!



Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 reaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • 1 ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix togetger flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.

  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixure into the flour mixture; beat until blended.

  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Sereve immediately.
